Empanadas

Description

Ingredients

  • (2) pounds of ground beef (feel free to use other meats such as ground chicken or turkey)
  • (1/4) cup of chopped onions
  • (1) packet of sazon
  • (3) tablespoons of sofrito
  • Adobo all purpose seasoning
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic powder
  • Black pepper
  • (2-3) tablespoons Canola oil
  • (1) can of tomato sauce
  • (2) packs of Empanada dough disks (10 per pack) (Make sure you defrost it the day before)
  • Olives (this is optional)

Steps

  1. Heat up your pan at medium heat. Once heated, add the canola oil
  2. Start with the onions and saute them for 2 mins
  3. Add the sofrito. Stir the ingredients together.
  4. Add the can of tomato sauce and packet of sazon. Stir together
  5. Add the meat. Mix the meat up and make sure all the ingredients are mix together.
  6. Add Adobo seasoning,Add the Onion powder, Garlic powder, and Black pepper. Stir all the ingredients together)
  7. Cover the pan and put the heat to medium-low. Check the beef in about 15-20 mins
  8. Roll empanada disks on a lightly floured surface until 1/2 inch larger in diameter. Spoon about 1 tablespoon meat mixture into the middle of each disk. Moisten the disk edges with water, fold in half over filling to form a half-moon, and pinch to seal (or seal with a fork)
  9. Heat 2 1/2 inches corn oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Working in batches, fry empanadas until crisp and golden brown, flipping once, 4 to 6 minutes. Transfer to paper towels to drain.
